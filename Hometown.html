Welcome to Ahmedabad!

City Information

  • State: Gujarat
  • Country: India
  • Population: 8,253,000
  • Mayor: Kirit Parmar

Ahmedabad, also known as Amdavad, was named after Sultan Ahmed Shah, who founded the city in 1411. It served as the capital of Gujarat from 1960 to 1970.

Some of the major tourist attractions of Ahmedabad are:

  • Roza
  • Calico Museum of Textiles
  • Sabarmati Ashram
  • City Museum
  • Colonel Shah Pir Ahmed
  • Kite Museum
  • Dada Harir Vav

Major Sports Team

  1. Gujarat Titans (Cricket)
  2. Gujarat Giants (Kabaddi)
  3. ARA FC (Football)
